Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly within the Love Peace Harmony app.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the Love Peace Harmony app
  2. Go to your Profile section
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Follow the confirmation prompts

What gets deleted:

  • Your user account
  • All milestone data
  • Saved preferences
  • All personal information

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
